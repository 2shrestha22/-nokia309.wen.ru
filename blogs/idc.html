Installing Darkman Certificate


1. First install nokia suite on your pc(download here). Connect your mobile. After your mobile is connected to nokia suite exit nokia suite but don't disconnect your mobile. We only need connectivity from nokia suite. Don't run nokia suite even in background, otherwise MobiMB can't detect your mobile.

2) Install MobiMB(download trial version from here), it is trial version so you should reinstall if not working. Start MobiMB.

3) After MobiMB detect your mobile it shows your C:/ drive. Open C:///predefmenuapps/certificates/user/

4) Download Darkman Certificate from here. Extract exp.cer and ext_info.sys and copy in that folder.

5) Exit MobiMB and restart mobile.

6) Go to setting>seciurity>user certificates and see there exp.cer which is Darkman Certificate.

7) Darkman certificate is installed and enjoy.

8) To sign your app with Darkman go here.

Note: Don't delete any other files/folders by MobiMB otherwise it will damage your mobile.
